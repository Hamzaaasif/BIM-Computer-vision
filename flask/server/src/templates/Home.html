Some General Tips 

Please follow all the guides to get your model accurate, this is an academic project and we want to take it to the future product. It is possible that you will not get the accurate results but don't worry this is just a demo :)
CLEAR AND BOLD IMAGES

Please upload a clear and bold images, the walls on the images are bold, not too thin lines not blurry images, and the are in jpg/jpeg format

Floor plans

For now, we are dealing only floor plans. The model will not detect the whole building 2D plan. Upload only 2D floor plan drawings

Less complex

This project uses machine learning algorithm to detect the 2D images, for now model is not too much accurate. try to use less noisy images

About Us

Our mission and goals

Just over an year ago, we became fascinated by the idea of seeing 2D images as 3D model. Throughtout the history of usiness, people use data to make informed decision. We decide to make impact on the construction industry. We believe that our project will generate a big imapact on the industry.

Mission

Our mission is to make planning a very simpler and easier process. Our simulations will help all the people. Professional architect, designers or even consumers can generate 3D models for better visualization.

Future Goals

Currently simulation is only for 3D models that allow contributers to understand the amount of volume and spaces in all the rooms and the proximity and relation between rooms. In future, we will include the functionality of interior designing. We will include the simulation of other relavent things regarding to planning like electrical drawing, plumbering drawings as well as cost estimation of the project
